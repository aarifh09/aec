What We Do

Core Engineering
Disciplines

Six integrated disciplines, one coordinated design team. We engineer every system in a building to work together — efficiently, safely, and sustainably.

01
HVAC Design
Load calculation, system selection, duct sizing, and energy optimization for all building typologies using ASHRAE methodology and latest simulation tools.
VRF / VRV Chiller Plants AHU / FCU HAP Analysis
02
Plumbing & Drainage
Domestic water supply, hot water systems, drainage, rainwater harvesting, and specialized medical plumbing designed to NBC 2016 and international standards.
NBC 2016 IS 1172 Heat Pump DHW
03
Electrical Distribution
LT power distribution, SLD design, cable sizing, transformer and DG sizing, earthing systems, and power quality analysis per IEC and IS standards.
SLD Design LT Cables DG / UPS IS 732
04
Fire Protection
Sprinkler systems, hydrant networks, fire suppression, detection and alarm systems designed to NFPA 13/14/20/72 and NBC 2016 with hydraulic calculation.
NFPA 13 Sprinklers Hydrant Detection
05
Building Automation & ELV
BMS/SCADA integration, CCTV, access control, structured cabling, public address, and smart building automation for energy management and operational control.
BMS/BAS SCADA ELV Systems
06
Energy Modeling & BIM
Energy simulation, sustainability consulting, LEED/GRIHA advisory, and full BIM coordination through Revit MEP and Navisworks clash detection workflows.
Revit MEP Navisworks LEED Net-Zero
